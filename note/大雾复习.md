### 大学物理公式

#### 第二章：牛顿运动定律

- **火箭方程**
$$
\vec{F} = m \frac{\mathrm{d}\vec{v}}{\mathrm{d}t} - \vec{u} \frac{\mathrm{d}m}{\mathrm{d}t}
$$

- **转动系惯性力**

$$
\begin{cases}
  \vec{F}_c = m\omega^2 \vec{r} \\
  \vec{f}_c = 2m\vec{v}' \times \vec{\omega}'
  \end{cases}
$$

- **角动量**

$$
\vec{L} = \vec{r} \times \vec{p} = \vec{r} \times (m\vec{v})
$$

- **力矩**

$$
\vec{M} = \vec{r} \times \vec{F}
$$


#### 第三章：刚体
- **角量与线量**

$$
\begin{cases}
  v = r\omega \\
  a_t = r\alpha \\
  a_n = \omega^2 r = \frac{v^2}{r}
  \end{cases}
$$

- **定轴转动定律**

$$
M = J\alpha
$$

- **平行轴定理**

$$
J_A = J_C + m\left(\frac{L}{2}\right)^2
$$

- **转动动能**

$$
E_k = \frac{1}{2}J\omega^2
$$

- **转动角动量**

$$
L = J\omega
$$


#### 第四章：流体运动
- **连续性方程**

$$
\rho_1 S_1 V_1 = \rho_2 S_2 V_2
$$

- **伯努利方程**

$$
P + \rho gh + \frac{1}{2}\rho V^2 = \text{常量}
$$


#### 第五章：狭义相对论
- **洛伦兹时空变换**

$$
\beta = \frac{v}{c}, \quad \gamma = \sqrt{1 - \beta^2}
$$


$$
\begin{cases}
  x' = \gamma(x - vt) \\
  y' = y \\
  z' = z \\
  t' = \gamma\left(t - \frac{\beta x}{c}\right)
  \end{cases}
$$


$$
\begin{cases}
  x = \gamma(x' + vt') \\
  y = y' \\
  z = z' \\
  t = \gamma\left(t' + \frac{\beta x'}{c}\right)
  \end{cases}
$$

- **原时最短**

$$
\Delta t = \gamma t_0
$$

- **原长最长**

$$
L = \frac{L_0}{\gamma}
$$

- **相对论动量**

$$
\vec{p} = m\vec{v}' = \frac{m_0}{\gamma}\vec{v}'
$$

- **相对论动能**

$$
E_k = mc^2 - m_0c^2 = \gamma m_0c^2 - m_0c^2
$$

- **总能量**

$$
E = E_k + m_0c^2 = mc^2 = \gamma m_0c^2
$$

- **相对论能量与动量关系**

$$
E^2 = p^2c^2 + m_0^2c^4
$$

### 第六章 气体动理论

- **理想气体状态方程**

$$
PV = \nu RT = n k T
$$


- **压强公式**

$$
P = \frac{1}{3} m_f n \overline{v^2} = \frac{2}{3} n \overline{\varepsilon}
$$


- **分子平均平动能**

$$
\overline{\varepsilon_t} = \frac{1}{2} m_f \overline{v^2}
$$


- **温度的微观解释**

$$
\overline{\varepsilon_t} = \frac{3}{2} k T
$$


- **分子的平均总动能**

$$
\overline{E_k} = \frac{1}{2} k T
$$


- **分子的平均能量**

$$
\overline{E} = \overline{E_k} = \frac{1}{2} k T
$$


- **内能改变**

$$
\Delta E = \frac{1}{2} \nu R \Delta T
$$


- **速率分布函数**

$$
f(v) = \frac{N}{V} \frac{dN}{dv}
$$


- **三种统计速率**

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \text{最概然速率} & v_p &= \sqrt{\frac{2RT}{M}} \\
& \text{平均速率} & \overline{v} &= \sqrt{\frac{8RT}{\pi M}} \\
& \text{方均根速率} & \sqrt{\overline{v^2}} &= \sqrt{\frac{3RT}{M}}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 第七章 热力学基础

- **热力学第一定律**

$$
Q = \Delta E + A
$$


- **摩尔定容热容**

$$
C_{V,m} = \frac{i}{2} R
$$


- **摩尔定压热容**

$$
C_{P,m} = \frac{i+2}{2} R
$$


- **等容过程分析**

$$
\Delta E = Q = C_{V,m} \Delta T = \nu \cdot \frac{i}{2} R \Delta T
$$


- **等压过程分析**

$$
C_{P,m} = C_{V,m} + R
$$


$$
Q = A = \int_{V_1}^{V_2} P \, dV = \nu RT \int_{V_1}^{V_2} \frac{dV} {V}= \nu RT \ln \frac{V_2}{V_1}
$$


- **绝热过程分析**

$$
A = -\Delta E = - \nu C_{V,m} \Delta T = - \nu \cdot \frac{i}{2} R \Delta T = \frac{i}{2} (P_1 V_1 - P_2 V_2)
$$


$$
PV^\gamma = C, \quad \gamma = \frac{i+2}{i}
$$


- **卡诺热机效率**

$$
\eta = \frac{A}{Q_1} = 1 - \frac{Q_2}{Q_1} = 1 - \frac{T_2}{T_1}
$$


- **克劳修斯等式与不等式**

$$
\sum_{i=1}^n \frac{Q_i}{T_i} \leq 0
$$


- **熵增加原理**

$$
dS \geq \frac{dQ}{T}
$$
